Why I prefer Cats over dogs

I like cats more than dogs because they don't require as much attention as dogs. I have two dogs and I'm constantly nagged to play fetch with him, but my cat lays around and sleeps. Another thing that makes them cool is that they have a ton of variety. All cats are different as in no two are alike, one may be more playful than the other and the other one may be introverted Cats are also the perfect pet for a family with a newborn baby, cats aren't to wild, they are calm unlike their dog counterpart.
